About

A bit about me

I'm a researcher and developer working across XR, AI, and human-centered design. Before Cornell, I worked at LG CNS and Samsung SDI in Seoul, and at FIT:MATCH in the US — building experience across product development, data systems, and user-facing technologies.

My work asks how emerging technologies can be designed more intentionally — not just to function, but to foster agency, transparency, and meaningful interaction. I plan to pursue a PhD to deepen this line of inquiry.
